--- title: "Food Wheel Team Project" excerpt: "The Food Wheel Team Project is meant for those who have a hard time deciding what to eat. This mobile application utilizes MIT App Inventor Software to bring a smooth, visually appealing graphic to the user, while solving their hunger crisis.
" collection: portfolio --- I, along with a team developed a food picker wheel app designed to simplify meal selection. The app allows users to input a list of food options, then spin a virtual wheel to randomly select a meal. The user-friendly interface ensures a smooth experience, while the underlying algorithm ensures fairness in selection. This project showcases my skills in app development, problem-solving, and user experience design, as it addresses the common challenge of indecisiveness when choosing what to eat.
